Hi! I'm Patricia Frogoso, a web developer passionate about front-end design, AI, and user experience. I love building creative projects that bring ideas to life. I love creating interactive, user-friendly websites that blend technology and creativity. With experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UX/UI design, I aim to craft seamless digital experiences.

Why I Love Web Development

Web development is like digital magic—I type some code, and suddenly, an entire website comes to life! I love the mix of creativity and problem-solving, whether it’s making an interface look sleek, debugging a stubborn issue, or experimenting with AI-driven designs. Every project feels like a fun puzzle (and yes, sometimes I do spend an embarrassing amount of time chasing down a missing semicolon). But the best part? Seeing an idea go from a simple sketch to a fully interactive experience that people actually use. It’s like art, but with more brackets and fewer paint stains!
