General Questions

What is a Mars rover?

A Mars rover is a robotic vehicle designed to explore the surface of Mars, equipped with cameras, sensors, and tools to study the planet's geology, climate, and search for signs of life.

Why is Mars so interesting to scientists?

Mars is the most Earth-like planet in our solar system. It may have once supported life, and studying it helps scientists understand planetary evolution and prepare for future human missions.

How many Mars rovers has NASA launched?

NASA has successfully sent five rovers to Mars: Sojourner, Spirit, Opportunity, Curiosity, and Perseverance.

Where can I follow NASA's Mars missions?

Visit the official NASA Mars Exploration website for mission updates, photos, and educational resources.
