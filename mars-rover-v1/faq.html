Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Mars rover?

A Mars rover is a motor vehicle designed to explore the surface of Mars. They are equipped with cameras, scientific tools, and sometimes even drones.

Why are we exploring Mars?

Mars may have once supported life. Understanding its geology and climate helps us learn more about the potential for life beyond Earth.
