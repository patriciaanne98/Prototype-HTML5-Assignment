Mission History

Launches, Landings, and Discoveries

Perseverance Rover

Launch Date: July 30, 2020
Landing Site: Jezero Crater – February 18, 2021
Mission Goals: Search for ancient microbial life, collect rock and soil samples.
Notable Tech: MOXIE, Ingenuity drone helicopter, Sample Caching System.

Curiosity Rover

Launch Date: November 26, 2011
Landing Site: Gale Crater – August 6, 2012
Mission Goals: Assess whether Mars ever had conditions suitable for life.
Notable Tech: ChemCam laser, onboard lab, radiation sensors.
