Mission History

Perseverance

Launch: July 30, 2020
Landing: February 18, 2021 at Jezero Crater
Goals: Search for signs of ancient life, collect rock samples.
Key Features: MOXIE oxygen generator, Ingenuity drone.

Curiosity

Launch: November 26, 2011
Landing: August 6, 2012 in Gale Crater
Goals: Determine if Mars ever had conditions for life.
Key Features: Chemistry lab on wheels, self-driving capability.
