Mission History

Launches, Landings, and Discoveries

Perseverance Rover

Launch Date: July 30, 2020
Landing Site: Jezero Crater – February 18, 2021
Mission Goals: Search for ancient microbial life, collect rock and soil samples.
Key Technology: Ingenuity helicopter, MOXIE oxygen generator, sample caching system.

Curiosity Rover

Launch Date: November 26, 2011
Landing Site: Gale Crater – August 6, 2012
Mission Goals: Study Mars' climate and geology, assess habitability for microbial life.
Key Technology: Laser ChemCam, radiation detectors, onboard science lab.
